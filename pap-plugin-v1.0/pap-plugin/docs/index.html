PAP

Your AI Chief of Staff coordinates a team of specialists that works around the clock — even while you sleep.

PAP is an always-on AI agent system built on Claude's Cowork platform. It automates real work for you, runs while you sleep, and gets smarter about what you actually want over time.

Install

In Claude Desktop, open the Cowork tab → Customize → Add Plugin, then paste:

github.com/pap-ai/pap

What it does

Builds custom tools

Tailored to how you work, through a guided discovery process.

Runs while you sleep

Scheduled tasks automatically deliver outputs each morning.

Captures and connects

A second brain for everything you read and watch.

Improves itself

Surfaces opportunities to work better as it learns about you.

Who it's for

PAP works well for you if you:

  • Have recurring tedious tasks worth automating
  • Are willing to spend an afternoon on initial setup
  • Are comfortable with basic account creation
  • Pay $20/month for tools that save you significant time
  • Have a spare computer to dedicate to running it

PAP isn't a great fit if you want something that works in 10 minutes, are intimidated by configuration, or don't have a spare computer.

What it costs

  • Claude Pro subscription: $20/month (required)
  • Hardware: one-time, $0 (existing computer) to $599 (new Mac Mini)
  • Optional services: typically free to $5/month

Most users spend $20-25/month total.

Privacy and security

  • Your data stays on your hardware and your accounts
  • No telemetry sent to PAP project servers
  • All credentials in your password manager
  • The clean machine is isolated from your personal files
  • Open source — review the code yourself
